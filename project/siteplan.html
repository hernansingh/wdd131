Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to introduce myself and my YouTube channel, share my ideas, and provide an easy way for promoters to contact me. The site will help me connect with promoters who align with my principles while avoiding collaborations with industries such as betting and casinos. Additionally, it will serve as a dynamic and professional representation of my content.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

How can I learn more about the content of this YouTube channel?

Scenario 2

How can promoters get in touch for collaboration opportunities?

Color Schema

The color scheme for the site will include:

  • Blue (#102c54) for headings and accents to convey trust and professionalism
  • White (#f5f5f5) for background and buttons to maintain a clean and modern look
